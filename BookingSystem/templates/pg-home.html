Bolinao Association of Tour Guides
(BATOG)

Bolinao Association of Tour Guides (BATOG) is an organization composed of freelance tour guides in Bolinao who are trained and accredited by the Department of Tourism. Each community guide has a field of specialization such as river guiding, falls guiding, cave guiding, and Giant Clam guiding.
